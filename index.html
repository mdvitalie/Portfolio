Case study

train4less

Train4Less is a website for a gym offering exercise at a lower price. We brainstormed names and came up with the name for the website as a group. It promotes an excellent price and excellent training services.

The website offer better and more personalized services for standard gym price and motivate visitors to revisit the page and lead them towards healthy lifestyle. It also offer free nutrition tips and collect email addresses of potential customers. The gym offer free trial to attract new lientele.

The aim of the website is to stand out from the competition in the area and continuously gain new clientele while also keep loyalty of existing client base. The website targets all healthy lifestyle enthusiasts, anybody who is training at home, anybody who wishes to join a gym and also anybody who appreciate stronger personal approach.

My Tasks on the Website

I did the sketching of all wireframes in Adobe Photoshop for mobile and Desktop. I also drew the mobile and desktop wireframe for Contact us and Free trial pages as well as site map and contact form and mobile menu to pages. Finally, I also built Contact us and Free trial pages.
